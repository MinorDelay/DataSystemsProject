Exploring coinciding factors of terrorism

The current dashboard is meant for policymakers who would like to explore factors coinciding with terrorism. The video below will tell you about relevancy and usability of the current dashboard.
In each tab of the dashboard a part the dashboard is shown. The tab "Sociodemographics and terrorism" shows the factors coinciding with terrorism and their respective values. The tab "Vehicle rammings and trends" displays how the vehicle rammings that started in the region of Israel and Gaza/Westbank transferred to Western-Europe.
